Experience

Senior Design Project - Aug 2015 to May 2016

I worked under the guidance of the Bretl Research Group to improve the functionality and sensory feedback of a prosthetic arm through iterative design and extensive experimentation.

We implemented barometric pressure sensors into fingertips in order to measure force during interactions, and conveyed corresponding information to the user through electrotactile stimulation via electrodes attached on the upper arm.

Our project won two awards: Best Senior Design Presentation - Judges' Choice and Best Senior Design Vendor Display.
